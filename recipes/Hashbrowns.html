Hash browns

Description

Good, old-fashioned, restaurant-style hash browns. Perfect with hot pepper sauce and ketchup!

Ingredients

  • russet potatoes
  • medium onion
  • all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • frying oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear
  2. Heat oil, place potatoes cover whole bottom of pan
  3. cook until nicely browned on bottom
  4. remove from pan, drain on paper towels.
