Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting.

It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and

adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich, decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles.

Serve this comfort-food macaroni and cheese casserole with grilled meats, sloppy Joes, fried chicken or alongside a salad for a meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings: salt and pepper/paprika
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. make the topping
  4. Bake the mac and cheese
